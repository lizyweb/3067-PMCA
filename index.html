Learn About Us

Postive Mind Care Academy

At Positive Mind Care Acadamy, we believe in the potential of every child. Our mission is to create a nurturing and inclusive environment where children with special needs can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We are dedicated to providing personalized education plans that cater to the unique learning styles and requirements of each student, ensuring they receive the support and encouragement they need to succeed.

  • Academic Support: Customized curriculum and teaching strategies to help students achieve their academic goals.
  • Therapeutic Services: On-site speech therapy, occupational therapy, and counseling to support students' developmental needs.
  • Social Skills Development: Programs designed to enhance communication, cooperation, and social interaction.

Our Services

Special Education

In our school, we are committed to creating an environment where every child can thrive. Our special education program is designed to foster academic, social, and emotional growth, ensuring that students with special needs are not only included but also valued for their unique contributions.

Learn More

Speech Therapy

Our school is committed to providing comprehensive support to all students, including those with special needs. One of the key services we offer is speech therapy, tailored specifically for children who require assistance in developing their communication skills.

Learn More

Occupational Therapy

Occupational Therapy (OT) for special kids in our school is an integral part of fostering an inclusive and supportive educational environment. Our dedicated occupational therapists work closely with children who have various developmental, physical, and cognitive challenges to enhance their ability to perform everyday tasks and participate fully in school activities.

Learn More

Physio Therapy

Physical therapy for special kids in our school is an essential service designed to support the unique needs of students with physical and developmental challenges. Our dedicated team of trained physical therapists works closely with children to improve their mobility, strength, and coordination through personalized therapy plans.

Learn More

Neuro Development Therapy

Neurodevelopmental Therapy (NDT) is a transformative approach we proudly offer at our school, designed to support children with special needs in their cognitive, motor, and social development. NDT focuses on enhancing the nervous system's ability to process and respond to sensory stimuli, which is crucial for the development of functional skills.

Learn More

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a highly effective therapeutic approach that can be adapted to meet the needs of special kids in school settings. CBT focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors, with the goal of improving emotional regulation and coping skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a special kids' school?

    A special kids' school is a learning environment specifically designed to meet the educational needs of children with special needs or disabilities. These schools provide individualized support, adapted curriculum, and specialized services to help children reach their full potential.

  • What types of disabilities or special needs do these schools cater to?

    Special kids' schools cater to a wide range of disabilities and special needs, including autism spectrum disorders, ADHD, learning disabilities, intellectual disabilities, sensory processing disorders, and physical disabilities, among others.

  • What kind of support do special kids receive at these schools?

    Special kids receive comprehensive support at these schools, including individualized education plans (IEPs), adaptive learning materials, assistive technology, therapy services, and social skills training. The goal is to address their specific challenges and help them succeed academically, socially, and emotionally.

  • What makes Positive Mind Care Academy unique?

    Positive Mind Care Academy Special Kids School is unique in its approach to education, focusing specifically on the needs of children with special needs. Our personalized learning plans, inclusive environment, and dedicated staff set us apart and ensure every child receives the support they need to thrive.

  • How are students' progress and goals tracked and communicated to parents?

    We use a variety of assessment tools and data tracking systems to monitor students' progress and track their goals. Progress reports are regularly shared with parents, and parent-teacher conferences are held to discuss individualized learning plans and set new goals. Open communication is key, and we encourage parents to reach out to us at any time with questions or concerns.

  • What qualifications do the teachers and staff at Positive Mind Care Academy have?

    Our teachers and staff at Postive Mind Care Academy Special Kids School are highly qualified and experienced in working with children with special needs. They undergo specialized training in areas such as behavior management, assistive technology, and differentiated instruction to ensure they can effectively support every child in our care.
